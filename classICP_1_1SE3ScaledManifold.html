Detailed Description

Custom Ceres manifold for SE(3) with rotation scaling.

This manifold uses se3PlusScaled and plusJacobian7x6Scaled to implement the Plus operation with a configurable rotation scale parameter.

The scaling applies a characteristic length L to the rotation component:

\[ \delta' = S \delta, \quad S = \begin{bmatrix} I_3 & 0 \\ 0 & L I_3 \end{bmatrix} \]

This makes "1 unit of rotation (radian)" comparable to "L units of translation", improving conditioning when translation and rotation have very different scales.

The ambient space is 7D: [qx, qy, qz, qw, tx, ty, tz] The tangent space is 6D: [v_x, v_y, v_z, w_x, w_y, w_z]

Note
The final result is invariant to scaling; only convergence speed is affected.
